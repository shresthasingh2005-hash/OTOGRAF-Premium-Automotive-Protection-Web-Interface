## ✨ Features

- **Cinematic Hero Section** — Full-screen hero with autoplay background video and elegant scroll indicator
- **Luxury Dark Theme** — Gold & black color palette inspired by brands like McLaren, IWC, and Rolls-Royce
- **GSAP Scroll Animations** — Smooth reveal animations on scroll for sections, cards, and text
- **Product Showcase** — PPF, Ceramic Coating, and Colour PPF & Vinyl Dip with dedicated cards
- **Dropdown Navigation** — Multi-level product dropdown with hover-activated menus
- **E-Warranty System** — Dedicated warranty page for customers
- **Get A Quote CTA** — Prominent call-to-action for lead generation
- **Fully Responsive** — Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
